# Legal and Copyright Notes

This repository is public. This document records the copyright and liability reasoning behind how it handles sources.

## Copyright

| Material | Status | How we handle it |
|---|---|---|
| Queensland Acts and Regulations | Crown copyright (State of Queensland) | Cite and link to legislation.qld.gov.au. No full-text mirroring. Excerpts of 25 words or fewer only where wording is decisive. |
| OFT approved forms (Form 6, Form 2, Form 8) | Government copyright | Never commit the form file. Link to the official OFT download page. Rules describe field-level obligations in our own words. |
| ACCC guidance | Commonwealth copyright, generally Creative Commons | Cite and link. We still paraphrase rather than copy. |
| REIQ / law-firm articles | Third-party copyright, often member-only | Link only, registered as industry_commentary. Never reproduce. |

## Liability posture

- The tool is explicitly **not legal advice** (see `DISCLAIMER.md`), to reduce the risk of it being construed as unqualified legal practice.
- Accuracy is protected by the verify-or-flag rule: an unverifiable pinpoint is marked `unverified`, not guessed.
- Every citation is pinned to a version or date so stale law is not presented as current.
- The skill refuses when no cited rule covers a question, rather than improvising.

## Privacy

No client personal information enters the repo. See `docs/pii-and-private-data.md` and `SECURITY.md`.

## Not a law firm

This project does not provide legal services and creates no solicitor-client relationship. It is a structured reference layer over public sources, for use by licensed professionals who remain responsible for their own compliance.
