# Sources to Obtain, Link, or Avoid

This is the working list for whoever maintains the rules. It separates what you link to, what you download for your own reference, and what you must not copy.

## Download for your own reference only (gitignored, never commit)

Put these in `sources/_downloads/` (gitignored) so you can write accurate field-level rules:

- Form 6 — Appointment and reappointment of a property agent (OFT)
- Form 2 — Seller Disclosure Statement (under the Property Law Act 2023)
- Form 8 — where the buyer-disclosure angle is relevant

These are Government copyright. They inform the rules; they are not committed.

## Avoid entirely

- Paywalled or member-only REIQ content.
- Full text of any Act, Regulation, or form.
- Law-firm article text (link only, as industry_commentary).
- Any third-party PDF of the legislation.

## Note on verification

Every pinpoint cited in a rule must be checked against the live official source before the rule is marked `verified`. Section numbers and form fields are not written from memory.
