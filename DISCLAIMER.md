# Disclaimer

**This project is not legal advice.**

The Queensland Real Estate Compliance Assistant is an information tool for licensed real estate professionals. It organises and cites publicly available Queensland law and regulator guidance. It does not provide legal advice, and using it does not create a solicitor-client relationship.

## What this tool is

- A source-backed reference that answers Queensland property-sales compliance questions only from structured rule files, and cites the legislation, regulation, approved form, or regulator guidance behind each answer.
- A tool that refuses to answer when no cited rule covers the question.

## What this tool is not

- It is **not** a substitute for the Queensland Office of Fair Trading, the ACCC, or a qualified Australian legal practitioner.
- It is **not** a guarantee that any rule is current. The law changes. Every citation is pinned to a version or date, but you must verify against the official source before relying on it.
- It is **not** financial, tax, or insurance advice.

## Your responsibility

- Always verify a citation against the official source linked in the rule before you act on it.
- Where a rule is marked `verification_status: unverified`, `partially_verified`, or `needs_review`, treat it as a prompt to check, not as a settled answer.
- For anything with legal or financial consequence (commission recovery, contract termination, seller-disclosure defects, trust accounts, or an OFT complaint), seek advice from a solicitor or the OFT.

## Scope

Version 1 covers **Queensland property sales only**. It does not cover residential tenancies, property management, or other Australian jurisdictions.

By using this tool you accept that you remain responsible for your own compliance obligations.
